FMoIP Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

FMoIP ("we", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how we handle information when you use the FMoIP radio app.

Data We Collect

  • Locally stored data: Your selected country, favorite stations, recordings, and app preferences are stored only on your device.
  • Location: When you enable automatic country selection, we use your device location (approximate) once to suggest your country. This is not stored on our servers.
  • Advertising: We use Google AdMob to show ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for personalized advertising. You can manage ad personalization in your device settings or through the consent form shown in the app.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email, or phone number, except when you voluntarily contact us (e.g., via the feedback feature).

Third-Party Services

  • Radio Browser API: We fetch station lists from radio-browser.info. No personal data is sent.
  • Google AdMob: For ads and consent. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • In-app purchases: Handled by Google Play or Apple App Store. We do not store payment details.
