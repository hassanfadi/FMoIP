Privacy Policy for FMoIP

Last updated: April 2026

FMoIP ("the app") is a mobile application for discovering and streaming FM radio stations over the internet. This policy explains how we handle information in relation to the app.

We do not store your personal data

We do not operate servers that collect or store your personal data. We do not have user accounts, and we do not gather names, emails, or other identifying information from you. Any data the app uses is either kept only on your device or is handled by third-party services under their own policies (see below).

Data on your device

The following are stored only on your device and are not sent to us or stored by us:

  • Preferences: Language, theme (e.g. dark/light), list size, data saver, recording quality and mode.
  • Favorites: Your list of favorite stations and favorite recordings (stored locally).
  • Subscription status: Subscription status (whether you have an active in-app subscription) is managed by Google Play (on Android) or the App Store (on iOS). We do not run our own subscription or billing systems. The app only asks the store whether you have an active subscription and may store that result locally so it can hide ads without asking every time. We do not maintain a separate database of subscribers.
  • Recordings: Any voice recordings you make are saved only on your device. We do not have access to them.

We do not upload this data to our servers because we do not run servers that store customer data.

Location

The app may use your device's approximate location to suggest a country for the station list (e.g. to show stations relevant to your region). This is used only on your device to choose a default country. We do not collect, store, or transmit your location to our own systems.

Third-party services

The app uses services provided by others. Their use of data is governed by their own privacy and terms:

  • Google Play / App Store: App installation, updates, and in-app purchases (including subscriptions) are processed by Google or Apple. Their privacy policies apply to that processing.
  • Advertising (Google AdMob): If you use the free version of the app, ads may be shown via Google AdMob. Google's advertising and privacy policies apply: Google Privacy Policy.
  • Radio station data: The app uses publicly available information (e.g. station names, stream URLs, countries) from public sources to list radio stations. We do not control or produce this data.

Radio station content – not our responsibility

The app only helps you find and listen to streams from third-party radio stations. We do not create, host, or control the audio or any other content that those stations broadcast. All programming, music, talk, and any other content are the sole responsibility of the respective radio stations and their operators. We are not responsible for the nature, accuracy, or legality of what third-party stations broadcast. If you have concerns about specific content, please contact the station in question.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will be revised when we do. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or the app, you can contact us through the support channel or contact method provided in the app listing (e.g. on Google Play or the App Store).
